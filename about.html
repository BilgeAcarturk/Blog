About Me

Developer & Designer

I am a computer engineering student at Kadir Has University, working on various software development projects to enhance my skills. Driven by my passion for cinema, I wanted to create a platform where I could share my personal thoughts and insights on the world of film. This led to the creation of CineEuphoria, a space where I open doors to the rooms in my mind, filled with reflections on the vibrant and diverse universe of cinema. My aim is to offer different perspectives and inspire others through the colorful world of movies.
